Features and Tips.
Delta-T Procedure.
You have to carry out two scans. All cavities between the cavity of interest and BPMs should be "switched off".  The first scan should be  done  with the cavity of interest in the "switched ON" state. The second one is performed with this cavity "switched OFF".
Phase Button on Scan Panel.
The Phase button can be used to specify that you scan over a phase variable. In this case a scan range will be wrapped to fit into from -180 to +180 degrees. For instance the scan from 160 to 210 will consist of two scans (+160)-(+180) and (-180)-(-150).
